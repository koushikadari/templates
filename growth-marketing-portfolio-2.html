Experimentation Lab

Test. Learn. Scale.

1,200+ experiments across 8 years. A sample of high-impact tests that moved the needle significantly.

01
Landing Page Headline Test
Hypothesis: Benefit-focused headlines outperform feature-focused headlines for conversion among mid-market buyers entering via paid search.
✓ +34% Conversion Lift
Impact: $2.4M annualized pipeline from reduced CPL on core acquisition campaigns.
02
Email Send-Time Optimization
Hypothesis: Personalized send-time delivery based on individual engagement history outperforms fixed 10 AM Tuesday cadence for open rates.
✓ +28% Open Rate
Impact: 41% increase in email-sourced pipeline across lifecycle nurture programs.
03
Pricing Page Architecture
Hypothesis: Three-tier pricing with a "most popular" visual anchor drives higher plan selection toward mid-tier vs. two-tier layout.
✓ +52% Upgrade Rate
Impact: $6.1M incremental ARR from improved pricing page conversion within 90 days.
04
Ad Creative Format Testing
Hypothesis: UGC-style video with authentic customer testimonials outperforms polished brand video for TOFU paid social at scale.
✓ 3.2X CTR
Impact: Reduced CPA by 44% and enabled 2X budget reallocation to scale winning creative.
05
Onboarding Flow Sequence
Hypothesis: Personalized onboarding path based on job function increases time-to-first-value and 30-day activation rate vs. single generic flow.
✓ +47% Activation
Impact: 34-point retention improvement at day-90 cohort, reducing churn-driven revenue loss by $3.8M.
06
Referral Incentive Structure
Hypothesis: Dual-sided cash incentive ($25/$25) outperforms single-sided credit for both referral initiation rate and referred user conversion quality.
✓ 2.8X Referrals
Impact: K-factor increased from 0.4 to 1.7, enabling self-sustaining organic growth loop.
