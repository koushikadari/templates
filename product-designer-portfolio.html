Design Philosophy

"The best design is the one users never notice — because it simply works."

I'm a Senior Product Designer based in San Francisco with a deep focus on healthcare and fintech products. My work sits at the intersection of user psychology, systems thinking, and visual craft.


Previously at Stripe, Figma, and a handful of early-stage startups, I've led design across web, iOS, and Android. I believe in ruthless simplicity, research-driven decisions, and shipping work that actually changes how people experience the world.
