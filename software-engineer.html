01 — About

A short introduction

A designer's bio, written the way I'd tell it across a table.

I started building websites in a small coastal town at fourteen, mostly to put my friends' bands online. Two decades later, the curiosity hasn't moved much — I'm still trying to make the screen feel like something you'd want to touch.

Today I partner with early-stage teams as a designer-engineer hybrid: shaping the product, writing the interface, and obsessing over the seams in between. My favorite work sits at the intersection of editorial design and software — fast, elegant, opinionated.

When I'm not at the desk you'll find me restoring an old film camera, swimming somewhere cold, or cooking for too many people.

— Alex
