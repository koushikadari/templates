About

Engineering ideas

I'm a Senior Mechanical Design Engineer with over 12 years of hands-on experience translating complex engineering challenges into manufacturable, high-performance solutions spanning aerospace, oil & gas, and industrial automation.

I specialize in thermodynamic system design, FEA/CFD simulation, and GD&T-driven manufacturing documentation. Every component I design is optimized for function, cost, and lifecycle — because good engineering is invisible when it works.

I believe precision is not a constraint but a creative discipline. I thrive in cross-functional teams where design, materials science, and production engineering intersect.
