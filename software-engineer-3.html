§ 05 · About

Briefly: who I am away from the keyboard.

I like systems you can hold in your head, code reviews that change your mind, and shipping the boring version first.

I grew up writing PHP guestbooks in a coastal town in Portugal, got serious in university, and have spent the last nine years split between fintech, developer tools and analytics. My favourite part of the job is the design phase — the hour with a whiteboard and a stale coffee where the shape of the thing actually gets decided.

Outside work I run slowly, cook ambitiously, and maintain a small open-source library that more people use than I expected. I'm a careful writer, a patient mentor, and an enthusiastic generator of small command-line tools that nobody asked for.

I'm currently happy at Northbeam, but I keep a short list of teams I'd consider — if you're working on developer tools, financial infrastructure or anything that involves a really hard data model, I'd love to hear about it.
