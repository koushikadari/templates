About Me

A Strategic HR Leader Who Believes in the Power of People

I'm Sarah Mitchell, a strategic HR professional with over 12 years of experience spanning financial services, technology, and retail. I specialise in designing HR frameworks that balance business objectives with genuine employee wellbeing.

My philosophy is simple: when people feel seen, valued, and challenged, organisations flourish. I've led transformational HR programmes across EMEA and partnered with C-suite executives to build cultures where performance and humanity coexist.

When I'm not redesigning onboarding journeys or coaching managers, you'll find me mentoring early-career HR professionals through the CIPD network.
