About Me

A storyteller with a camera.

I'm Lena — a photographer based in Lisbon with a soft spot for golden hour, honest emotion, and the small details others miss. For over a decade I've been telling stories through light, shadow, and silence.

My style blends documentary realism with editorial elegance. I work slowly and quietly, letting moments unfold rather than directing them. Every frame is an invitation to remember.
